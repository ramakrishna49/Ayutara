FAQs

Frequently asked questions.

Get instant clarity on Ayutara’s medical alerts, data privacy, customization, and how we support your practice with evidence-based updates.

Your Questions, Answered

How are alerts personalized for my specialty?

During onboarding, select your primary and secondary specialties. Our AI matches content from 100+ sources (NEJM, Lancet, ICMR, etc.) to your profile. You’ll only receive what’s relevant.

Is patient data ever involved?

No. Ayutara never accesses or stores patient data. We deliver general medical intelligence only. Fully compliant with HIPAA, GDPR, and DPDP Act.

Can I control alert frequency?

Yes. Choose from Real-Time, Daily Digest, or Weekly Summary. Pause or adjust anytime from your dashboard or email footer.

Do you include CME reminders?

Absolutely. Get automated reminders for accredited CMEs, webinars, and conferences with direct links and deadline alerts.

How do I unsubscribe?

Click “Unsubscribe” in any email or log in to your account. You can also pause alerts temporarily without losing your preferences.

Is there a mobile app?

Yes! Download Ayutara from the App Store or Google Play. Push notifications, offline reading, and voice summaries available.
